Moving information

Now we are still located at Kasteeldreef 122, 5046 CV Tilburg, we had an exceptional time there, which is stored in the history of this company, the place where everything started .. But time continues and so does the company, the building where we are now is too small to be able to work in a professional and decent way, also because the company is growing more and more and we are therefore getting more employees who need their own workplace, we do not have those privileges yet but We will get it soon, we will move to Schoolstraat 23, 4787 AH in Balkendam. This new building offers all the remaining privileges that the building we are in now does not offer us, it gives us more space to work well and the opportunity to hire more employees and therefore also interns, the move will take place on: Thursday April 1, 2021, that is already a good insight and so we still have to prepare a lot before it will happen, during this time we will be less accessible because we have to move a lot of stuff to the new building.
