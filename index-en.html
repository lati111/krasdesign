Introduction kras design

Greetings, we as in KrasDesign are a company that delivers what is asked. 20 years ago I (Jan) and Freek founded this beautiful company, we are very proud of what it has become. KrasDesign started as a small company that designed logos for private companies and freelancers. We eventually decided to get more out of this company, only logos were not enough for us, after brainstorming for a while I came to Freek with the idea of creating custom websites and certain corporate identities and eventually selling them to the buyer. This idea has made us who we are today, with the wide range of services we offer, we make every idea possible. That is ultimately what it is all about for KrasDesign.
